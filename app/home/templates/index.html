Beta Release - June 14, 2019

We are excited to release the beta version of Blue: the all-in-one web-based reconnaissance panel for penetration testers and bug bounty hunters.

Blue aims to make the reconnaissance period of a penetration test easier and quicker by having everything you need, in one place.

Blue currently supports:

  • Subdomain Enumeration
  • Google Dorking
  • Software Detection
  • Email Harvesting
  • Data Leak Lookups

Have a feature you'd like to see added to Blue? Click the contact option on the left underneath support and let us know!
